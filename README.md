*Outline their target learning audience*
The target learning audience for our project would be middle school students who are experiencing a myriad of behaviors in different academic settings within their school, and the educators who interact with them daily. Both parties are interested in bridging the gap between behavior and crafting spaces where learning occurs. 

*An identified learning need (with references, or personal experiences)*
The learning need arises from experiences such as the Hengshui model, which emphasized militaristic teaching and hyper fixation on testing. In our project, we would like to focus more on meeting students where they are and giving them the tools to succeed in a system that expects otherwise. 

*A rationale for how you imagine AI may assist this learning need*
* Personalized Feedback
* Student-created personas to track “character skills” - shifts in behavior
